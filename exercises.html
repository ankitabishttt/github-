Arm Exercises

  • Bicep Curls: Use dumbbells to perform curls for biceps.
  • Tricep Dips: Use a bench or chair to perform dips.

Leg Exercises

  • Squats: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and squat down.
  • Lunges: Step forward with one leg and lower your hips.

Core Exercises

  • Planks: Hold a plank position for as long as possible.
  • Sit-ups: Perform sit-ups to strengthen your core.

Back Exercises

  • Pull-ups: A great exercise for building overall back strength, particularly the lats.
  • Bent-over Barbell Row: This compound exercise targets the lats, rhomboids, and trapezius muscles.
