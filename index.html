Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the best diet for weight loss?

A balanced diet with a caloric deficit, including plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains, is effective for weight loss.

2. How often should I exercise?

It is recommended to exercise at least 150 minutes per week, including both aerobic and strength training activities.

3. Can I lose weight without exercising?

Yes, weight loss can be achieved through diet alone, but combining diet with exercise is more effective for long-term results.
